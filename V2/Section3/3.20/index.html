Concepts from this lesson:

  1. Attributes
    1. Any number of attributes may be attached to a HTML element.
    2. They must be within the opening tag.
    3. They must be separated by a space.
    4. They must have a name, followed by an equal sign =, followed by a value.
  2. Ordered list attributes:
    1. Start: sets the number or equivilent to the value of the attribute. start="3". Must be an integer regardless of the type.
    2. Type: sets the numbering type, such as roman numerals, letters, upper or lower case etc.
    3. Reversed: this boolean specifies that the list's items are in reverse order. items will be numbered from high to low.
